Welcome to George’s Place



Founded in the 90’s by my Dad, George. The renowned neon sign, gifted by my Mom, has traveled all across the Midwest; from bachelor pads, to suburban basements, to fraternity houses, and finally nestled inside the renowned Southport Corridor overlooking Wrigley Field. George’s Place is a historical cornerstone of the community and is a frontrunner in advocating for “the late nights”. We are here to provide media we know you will enjoy.
